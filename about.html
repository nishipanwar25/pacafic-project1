WE ARE PIEM

BEST PLACE TO START YOUR CAREER TO GROW UP

Pacific Institute of Engineering and Management is an autonomous body that was established in 1995. PIEM renders a path to early success in life through its incredible Online Distance Education Programs. PIEM is keeping in imparting Online Distance Education in the areas of Management, Engineering, Computers and ITI across the globe for working Professionals. PIEM consists of concepts, case studies and Industrial training that give its students a broad exposure to relevant business concepts, ideas and management specifics, which in turn helps the students to maximize their productivity. This helps the students in imparting them capability to formulate business policies, strategies and their implications by making them the best in their respective fields. PIEM’s short terms fast track programs are specifically designed for career-oriented professionals. The course design, study material, examination pattern and the grading system help the students in enhancing their skills and knowledge in every field of specialization. We at PIEM are committed to develop and educate individuals who become skillful leaders and who will make a difference both in the outside and business world. Since, the achievement of our alumni in different organizations and ranks of leaderships are reflections of this commitment. Our institute is also committed to foster professional development through rigorous institutional excellence with exposure to real business experiences.

At the Pacific Institute of Engineering and Management learning is a fun and interactive engagement. The experience goes beyond merely providing skills, tools or knowledge. It also builds up articulate thinking and judgment, which is the basis of a lifetime of leadership in a challenging and rapidly changing world.

The key benefit of PIEMs online distance education programs is in its flexibility of learning anytime, anywhere from across the globe with no age barriers. The curriculum provides with great academic knowledge combined with International skillset and competence to enable the candidate to build successful careers.
