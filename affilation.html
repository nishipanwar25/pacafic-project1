NITI Aayog (Govt. of India)

The National Institute for transforming India (NITI Aayog) Govt. of India DL/2018/0205044 has registered Pacific Education Trust Registration No. 395/2016-17...

According to Govt of India, National Education Policy 1986, Education Trust/Voluntary institute/Autonomous institute have right to spread distance education and issue certification on behalf of Degree and Diploma.

Pacific Institute of Engineering and Management is an autonomous body that was established in 1995. PIEM renders a path to early success in life through its incredible Online Distance Education Programs.
